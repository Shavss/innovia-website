Font and Colour Palettes

Five fonts and nine color directions for Innovia Partners.

All palettes are designed to meet WCAG 2.2(Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) contrast requirements. It's an internationally recognised standard published by the W3C. Primary text on background combinations target a minimum 4.5 : 1 contrast ratio (AA level for normal text), and large headings maintain at least 3 : 1 (AA level for large text). Accent colours are paired with light or dark surfaces to preserve legibility at every scale. These thresholds ensure readability for users with low vision or colour deficiencies and align with accessibility legislation including the European Accessibility Act (EAA), US Section 508, and UK Equality Act 2010.
